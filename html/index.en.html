Experience will help their future

All you had many experience in your life time, sometimes it was interesting, or also was difficult we guess.
But from these, you could be matured, notice new things, from encountering new people, we could have unexpected special connection, as we were, we would like to give children these oppotunities. In addition to the usual environment of children, we hope they can have chance to spend time with overseas children who can not meet easily.
Through it, if they can be naturally interested in culture and languages of other countries, when they have difficulties in their life in future, experiences expand their ideas and get to have the flexibility to think about, if we can help that, that is our wish.
